About Us

Welcome to the ABC Club! We are a group of students passionate about Android development. Whether you are a beginner who just wrote your first "Hello World" or an expert building complex apps, there is a place for you here.


We meet weekly to code, share ideas, and eat pizza. Join us to learn Kotlin, Java, and how to publish your own apps on the Play Store.
